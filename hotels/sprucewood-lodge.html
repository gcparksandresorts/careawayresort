Globe-City's Sprucewood Lodge

Inspired by the wonder and grandeur of nature, the Sprucewood Lodge combines the sophistication and comfort of a world-class resort with the beauty and serenity of the outdoors. Enjoy deluxe suites designed to look and feel like a luxurious cabin nestled in a dense sprucewood forest.
