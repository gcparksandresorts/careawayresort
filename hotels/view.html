Exclusive On-site Resort Benefits

Stay Connected to the Action

All five of our resort hotels and three parks are connected via the Shops & More District. Staying on-site means you have access to the entire resort, no car necessary.

Complimentary Transportation

For those wanting a easy-breezy trip to the parks, complimentary bus transportation is offered throughout the resort including to and from afiliate hotels and the Puertas Nuevas International Airport.

Be Carefree and Carry-Free

Guests shopping at select stores within the theme-parks and the resort shopping district can have their purchases delivered directly to their hotel room, no carrying required. Look for posted signage at participating locations.

Enjoy one more hour of play

Breeze into one of our theme parks up to an hour early with Early Park Admission. (Valid theme park admission required. Select attractions.)
