A look back at the opening day of Globe-City's Careaway Resort

Hugh G. Member

May 28, 2025

Today, Globe-City's Careaway Resort stands as one of Puertas Nuevas' most attended, sought for, and popular attraction. The resort has a heafty footprint with two theme parks, a waterpark, five resort hotels, and the iconic mile of shopping and dining fun.

But how did we get here? Though it may seem surprising, the Careaway Resort's first few months of operation were, to put it lightly, chaotic and literally explosive (more about that later). So, grab a nice cup of coffee from Mr. Teddy's Coffee Corner and let's get into the fun history of PN's favorite place.
