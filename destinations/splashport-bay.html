Important Guidelines for Safe Splashing

  • Metal objects including keys, loose change, jewlery, etc. are not permited in pool areas and flumes. These items must be left in a locker or with a guest waiting on the shore.
  • All guests visiting Splashport Bay must be free from the following conditions:
      Active diareah, open wounds, recent surgery, pregnant, chlorine sensitivity, sun sickness, skin rash, bacterial infections, conditions prohibiting swimming or entering pools
  • Guests sensative to saltwater, chlorine, or other aqautic-sanitization compounds should remain out of the pools and flumes.
  • We are not responsible for water damage to personal property including cell phones and electronic devices.
  • Stay hydrated! and waterproof sunscreen is highly recomended.
