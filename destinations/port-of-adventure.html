Journey Into Four Unique Lands

Port of Entry

A vibrant turn-of-the-century harbor bustling with excitement. With its grand lighthouse, charming cobblestone streets, and bustling market stalls, this welcoming gateway invites adventurers of all ages to set sail on grand journeys into lands of wonder, mystery, and thrills.

Wonder-Reel Studios

Lights, camera, action! Wonder Reel Studios brings the excitement of Hollywood to life with high-octane thrills and dazzling entertainment. Experience the rush of an action-packed stunt ride, marvel at Broadway-style productions in a glamorous theater, and immerse yourself in the glitz and behind-the-scenes magic of the silver screen.

Kingdom of Darkness

A sinister realm where malevolent villains and shadowy spirits reign. Wander through a decrepit castle and twisted dark forests filled with eerie whispers and glowing eyes. This foreboding land offers spine-chilling encounters and thrilling adventures for those brave enough to face the darkness.

Palace of Atlantia

A magical city once submerged beneath the waves, now risen to the surface. Explore shimmering palaces adorned with intricate mosaics, encounter mythical sea creatures, and uncover the mysteries of this aquatic utopia. With its glowing waterways and ethereal beauty, Atlantia is a realm of wonder waiting to be explored.

Broadway-level "Whoa"

In a league of its own, Port of Adventure proudly introduces the Sasson Theater, the crown jewel of Wonder Reel Studios. This state-of-the-art 1200-seat venue features a nearly three-story stage equipped with cutting-edge projection mapping, an advanced fly system, and the latest theatrical innovations. The Sasson Theater redefines live entertainment, transporting you into the heart of the story like never before.
