Forget Reality and Step Into Imagination

Careaway Park was created to be a place where adults and children alike can leave the real world behind and step into extraordinary realms of imagination. Set off on grand adventures through mystical temples, meet friendly creatures in musical towns, and discover snowy mountains, serene lakes, and faraway planets. With endless places to explore, your visit to Careaway Park is a journey into the heart of enchantment and adventure.
