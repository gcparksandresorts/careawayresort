Delivery Methods

Choose from the following methods for your Gift Ticket to be delivered

  • MCPS Standard Mail - Will be shipped in an envelope with MCPS at standard rate. Delivered within Minecraftia within 1-3 weeks from purchase. Included in standard price.
  • MCPS Express Mail - Will be shipped with MCPS at express rate, delivered within Minecraftia within 1 week from purchase. Additional fee required.
  • Resort Hotel Delivery - We will deliver the envelope within 24 hours to a specified hotel room at one of the 5 resort hotels on-property. Delivery method is offered free of charge.
  • Pickup - Order the envelope to be picked up from select locations within the resort free of charge. Email and text notifications will be sent when ready for pickup, free of charge.
