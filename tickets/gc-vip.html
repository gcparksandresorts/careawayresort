Benefits Details

Priority Queue Access

Priority Queues can greatly shorten your attraction waittimes with exclusive access to GC VIP ticketholders. Restrictions apply including limited uses per-attraction, 'cool-down' time periods between uses, and time-of-day restrictions. For more information on restrictions and limitations of priority queue access, view our GC VIP Terms and Conditions

Entertainment Priority Seating

For our live entertainment and nighttime spectaculars, GC-VIP guests with Deluxe and Platinum tickets can enter our priority seating areas for a better view of the show without the hassle of reserving a seat. Priority seating areas open 1-hour before showtime. Priority Seating areas are subject to capacity limitations.

VIP Lounges

Our exclusive VIP lounges feature comfortable indoor seating away from theme park crowds. Vending machines, water refill stations, and complimentary WIFI are also available. GC-VIP guests with Deluxe and Platinum tickets can access our VIP lounges by scanning their tickets at the entrance. Lounge space is first-come first-serve and some lounges may be closed or unavailable during your visit. To find VIP lounges, use a park guide map or the GC RESORT mobile app.

Preffered Parking

GC VIP Admission does not include parking, however, guests visiting with pre-purchased GC VIP Admission can show their tickets at the parking booth for a 10% Discount on Preffered Parking in theme-park parking structures only.

Complimentary GC Photo Pass

GC VIP Deluxe and Platinum admission includes GC PhotoPass free-of-charge. To activate, download the GC RESORT mobile app and set up GC PhotoPass using your GC VIP ticket ID.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I redeem the complimentary GC PhotoPass

GC VIP Deluxe and Platinum admission and upgrades include complimentary GC PhotoPass for unlimited downloads of all your ride photos and in-park photos. To access this feature, download the GC RESORT mobile app, press "Link Tickets" then scan the barcode on the back of your admission tickets. Once linked, visit the GC PhotoPass module to link ride photos, linking your ticket should automatically unlock access to this feature.

If I upgrade my admission to GC VIP, can I upgrade again to a higher tier later?

Yes, all admission tiers are eligible to upgrade to a higher tier of GCVIP privileges. However, certain tiers may be sold-out during your visit. Consult Guest Services or any in-park information booths for details.

If I am visiting with an Annual Passport, can I still upgrade to GC VIP Privileges?

Yup! You can purchase for-the-day GC VIP privileges at Will Call, Guest Services, or any in-park information booths. You can also upgrade your annual passport to the Platinum VIP Passport at Guest Services for 12 months of VIP privileges.

How do I know how much time is left on my cool-down period after using my GC VIP priority queue access?

Currently, the only way to check if your pass is valid is to visit any priority queue entrance. Once your ticket is scanned, the screen will display how much time you have remaining on your cool-down period.

What if I lose my GC VIP paper ticket?

Once your ticket is printed, we encourage you to download the GC RESORT mobile app and link your tickets right away! This way you will always have a digital version of your ticket. If you lose your paper ticket, they can be reprinted at any in-park information booth, guest services, or Will Call. Either your paper ticket or your digital ticket are requried to enter the prirority queues and lounges.
