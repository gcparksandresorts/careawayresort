IMPORTANT INFORMATION

This event is not recommended for children under 16 years of age

Alcoholic Beverage Sales

This event serves alcohol - All guests MUST be 21 years of age or older to purchase and consume alcoholic beverages. Outside food & drink is strictly prohibited. Guests cannot leave the event with alcohol whether or not purchased at the event. Heavily intoxicated guests will be required to recover at designated medical facilities. Possession and consuming of illicit drugs including marijuana on Globe-City Property is strictly prohibited, all violators will be removed from Careaway Resort property.

Parking and Transportation

To accomodate daily theme park traffic, special event guests will be required to park in the East Resort Parking Lot located east of Careaway Park. Complimentary shuttle services are provided. Guests who are able to are encouraged to commute to the event using public transportation at the Careaway Resort Transit Center.

Event Rules & Safety Guidelines

While Port Electric is considered an adult music festival, the grounds around the event are an operating family-friendly resort. Inapropriate attire and guest conduct will be prosecuted by Resort Security, especially that occuring outside the event in and around Resort Hotels.
