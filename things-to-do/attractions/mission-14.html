Know Before You Go

Mission 14 is a fast-paced rollercoaster in the dark. This ride features many sharp turns, sudden drops, unexpected stops, intense g-force, and flashing lights. It is advised that expectant mothers, guests with recent medical history, and guests with conditions which may be aggravated by this experience do not ride.
Guest Safety: Before your visit, review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to experiencing. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about your experience.
