Know Before You Go

FRANKENKOASTER is an intense outdoor launched rollercoaster. This ride feautures intense movevement and forces, multiple inversions, air time, steep drops, and hard stops. Expectant mothers and those with medical conditions which may be aggrevated by this experience should not ride. The restraints on this attraction may be restricting to larger guests.

Guest Safety: Before your visit, review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to experiencing. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about your experience.
