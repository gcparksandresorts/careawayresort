Centuries ago in the Kingdom of Transylvania there was a grand celebration following the Winter harvest. That night, as the fun lay away and the villagers began to make their beds, an evil darkness krept over the town.

As the citizens of Transylvania slept, this darkness entered their homes and their minds, purging their dreams into a nightmare realm where the evil vampire Dracula grabbed hold of their souls.

Relive the terrifying tale on this unique and innovative multi-dimension coaster. Unbound from the limits of traditional ride systems, the story of The Nightmare Dracula will grip you like never before. Will your soul survive the trial?





Know Before You Go

The Nightmare Dracula is an intense dark ride rollercoaster. This attraction features flashing lights, loud noises, fog effects, spinning motion, sharp drops, hard stops, backwards movement, and intense motion. Due to the nature of this attraction, expectant mothers and those with medical conditions which may be agrivated by this experience should not ride. The restraints on this attraction may be restricting to some guests.

If you have questions about this experience, feel free to connect with Attraction Operators at the entrance to the attraction. For your convinience, a test seat is available to guests unsure about their ability to comfortably experience this ride.


Guest Safety: Before your visit, review our Safety Guidelines and read all posted information at an attraction prior to experiencing. Please speak to attraction operators if you have questions or concerns about your experience.
